WHAT WE DO

We design experiences and environments for you to sense the world anew

Studio MoreThanThat is an artistic [agency / practise] designing multi-sensory experiences to tell stories. We incorporate touch, taste, smell, listening and seeing in different ways. Our work is a constellation between the mediums of performance art, immersive events, installations, film, architecture and a little more than that.
